FAQ / Legislation sections

The Mēk (Praeniy Naemal Seok) is the constitutional judge and representative of the state, serving as head of state under the Mekkism system of governance.

Elections are held every six months. All registered citizens participate via the NVote system. The Governor of the Senate is drawn from the coalition majority.

The Corona (CRN) is the official electronic centralised currency, administered via the Veronabank system.

Senate Decree No. 42

On the Digitalisation of the Corona Currency System

The Senate of the United States of Mekniy and Lurk, convened in ordinary session, hereby decrees the following measures regarding the standardisation and digital expansion of the Corona (CRN) currency under the administration of Veronabank.

Article I. The Veronabank system shall adopt the STEMMA API standard for all citizen-facing transactions effective the first day of the following quarter.

Article II. All citizens are hereby notified of their obligation to register with the electronic currency platform within sixty (60) calendar days of publication of this decree.

Praeniy Naemal Seok
Mēk of Mekniy-Lurk
Siwil Ara-den
Governor of the Senate
